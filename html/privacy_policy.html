The Poetic Hivemind:

Privacy

Policy

When you open the app for the first time, it generates a unique ID1 for you. This ID gets attached to all the poems that you submit and is the only personal information that is collected.

The app communicates with my server to submit poems and retrieve new daily themes. This generates the following data:

  • Masked IP-Address2 for privacy
  • Path sub part of the URL3 of your request
  • Date and Time of your request4
  • User-Agent5

I use this data to asses the status of the server. It will be deleted after 90 days.

  1. This is called a UUID. I use version 4. Further information on Wikipedia
  2. A numerical label assigned to your device. Reduced to 178.197.0.0 instead of 178.197.10.25 . Further information on Wikipedia
  3. The full URL would be https://tofu.wtf/poems/api/date/2020-02-12, the path is /poems/api/date/2020-02-12. Further information on Wikipedia
  4. 11/Feb/2020:15:24:42 +0000
  5. A string that identifies the software that was used to carry out the request. Example: Dart/2.8 (dart:io). Further information on Wikipedia

Usage —

The main purpose of the poems and IDs collected in this app is the creation of a training data set for an AI and for research. It is planned to publish the dataset at a point in the future.
Further uses can also include:

  • Exhibitions
  • Books

Deletion —

To reset your unique ID please remove the app and it's data from your phone and reinstall it. It will generate a new ID for your.

If you have any questions or would like all your data removed please write to me: poems@tofu.wtf

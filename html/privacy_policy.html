Privacy Policy —

When you open the app for the first time, it generates a unique ID for you. This ID gets attached to all the poems that you submit and is the only personal information that is collected.

The app communicates with my server to submit poems and retrieve new daily themes. This generates the following data:

  • Masked IP-Address
  • Path
  • Date and Time
  • User-Agent

I use this data to asses the status of the server. It will be deleted after 90 days.

Usage —

The main purpose of the poems and IDs collected in this app is the creation of a training data set for an AI and for research. Further uses can also include:

  • Exhibitions
  • Books

Deletion —

To reset your unique ID please remove the app and it's data from your phone and reinstall it. It will generate a new ID for your.

If you have any questions or would like all your data removed please write me poems@tofu.wtf
